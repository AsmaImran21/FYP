A platform to share ideas and innovation

Founded in 2024, our online platform is designed to bridge the gap between aspiring students and the wealth of knowledge from senior peers' projects.

Who We Are?

We are a dedicated group from the Department of Computer Science at GC Women University Sialkot. Our team consists of Meerab Mir, Asma Imran, and Eman Shahbaz, each of us carrying the passion for technology and innovation. Guided by our esteemed supervisor, Dr. Muhammad Hamid, we are committed to empowering future creators through our initiative, "Project Showcase to Empower Future Creators." Our platform is designed to bridge the gap between students and their aspirations by providing a comprehensive web platform that connects them with the experiences and projects of their senior peers.

Our Motivation

Our motivation stems from the challenges we observed among final year students in selecting and showcasing their projects. We noticed that a significant number of students struggle with finding inspiration and understanding the expectations for their Final Year Projects (FYPs). With around 70% of students facing issues in project selection, we saw an opportunity to make a difference. Our platform aims to facilitate idea generation, foster innovation, and enhance career prospects by showcasing student projects to a wider audience, including potential employers.
