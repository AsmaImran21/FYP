Frequently Asked Questions

What is 'Project Showcase to Empower Future Creators'? ▼

It's an online platform where students can submit and showcase their final year projects, fostering a community of innovation and collaboration.

Is there any fee associated with submitting a project?▼

No, project submission is completely free of charge.

Can employers access the project showcase to find potential hires? ▼

Yes, employers are encouraged to explore the showcase to discover talented individuals whose projects align with their company’s needs.

Can I edit or update my project details after submission?▼

No! The website aims to display one-time uploaded Final year Projects that are approved by supervisor. Any ammendments in projects are not accepted.

Is there a limit to the number of projects I can submit? ▼

While there is no strict limit, we encourage quality over quantity. Each project should be submitted only once. Moreover, in case of team-projects, only one memeber can upload the project.

Are international students’ projects included in the showcase? ▼

Yes, we welcome submissions from students around the globe to build an international community of future creators.
